What is PULSE?

PULSE is an innovative Predictive Health Management (PHM) application.

PULSE is a confidential, proprietary portal used by BeniComp to empower employees with healthy lifestyles, and lower healthcare costs by focusing on prevention.

Because health is a team effort, PULSE has various tools for:

  • Participants
  • Health Educators
  • Health Coaches
  • Clinical Directors

Predictive. Preventive. Actionable.

PULSE analyzes personal information and health history to identify health risks.

Employees receive actionable information needed to
practice prevention and achieve health goals.

Managing your health with PULSE is easy

  • Annual, comprehensive blood screenings are provided to predict serious but preventable health outcomes.
  • Employees securely access their health results anytime by logging into PULSE.
  • Health surveys and preventive test notifications are available to further control health.

Health Coaches use PULSE to:

  • Communicate with participants
  • Offer further education on screening results
  • Refer participants to a secondary level of care when necessary

At any time, participants can access:

  • Health plan documents
  • Appeal forms
  • Health tool kits
